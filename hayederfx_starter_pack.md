# Hayederfx — Instagram Starter Pack

Built around the 3-layer system: **Discovery** (Reels that reach strangers) → **Authority** (carousels people save) → **Proof** (your daily trade breakdowns). The breakdowns stay your anchor; the discovery + authority pieces are the growth engine you're currently missing.

---

## 1. Hook Bank (15 hooks across your 5 pillars)

A hook's only job is to stop the scroll in the first 1 second. Lead with intrigue, an outcome, a mistake, or a contrarian claim — never with a label like "trade breakdown." Say the hook out loud in the first frame *and* put it as bold on-screen text.

### Pillar 1 — Orderflow basics (your discovery layer — pulls beginners in)
1. "I ignored order flow for 2 years. Here's exactly what it cost me."
2. "Support and resistance is lying to you. Here's what actually moves price."
3. "If you only ever learn one order flow concept, make it this one."

### Pillar 2 — GEX / options flow (your differentiator — almost nobody teaches this well)
4. "Why NQ rejects the same level every single morning — and no, it's not random."
5. "Dealers told you where price would stop today. You just didn't know how to read it."
6. "The 'gamma flip' explained in 60 seconds — without the math degree."

### Pillar 3 — Trade breakdowns (your proof layer)
7. "Everyone watched NQ dump today. The tape called it 20 minutes early — frame by frame."
8. "+4R on NQ. The entry everyone scrolled past, broken down step by step."
9. "I almost skipped this trade. Then the order flow did *this*."

### Pillar 4 — Mistakes & psychology (your most shareable content)
10. "The order flow mistake that blew up my first funded account."
11. "You're not losing because of your strategy. You're losing because of this."
12. "3 order flow 'signals' that are actually traps — I fell for all of them."

### Pillar 5 — Process & system (your save-able authority content)
13. "My exact pre-market routine before I touch NQ. Steal it."
14. "How I journal every trade in under 5 minutes (and why it doubled my consistency)."
15. "The 1-page checklist I run before every single entry."

> **Tip:** Hooks 10, 11, 12 (mistakes) and 13, 14, 15 (process) are your highest-save / highest-share material. Make these carousels. Hooks 1–9 work best as Reels.

- Spend 20–30 min/day commenting thoughtfully on bigger order flow / futures accounts. This is half your growth.

## 3. Lead Magnet (your comment-to-DM growth lever)

This is the single fastest way to manufacture DM shares (Instagram's #1 ranking signal) *and* start building an email list you actually own. The mechanic: a Reel/carousel ends with "Comment **TAPE** and I'll DM you my free order flow cheat sheet" → people comment → you (or an auto-DM tool like ManyChat) send the resource.

### Recommended first resource: "The Order Flow Cheat Sheet" (1-page PDF)
Buildable in an afternoon. Contents:
- The 5 order flow signals you actually trade (absorption, exhaustion, delta divergence, stacked imbalances, trapped traders) — one line each
- A simple "what it looks like on the footprint" mini-diagram for each
- Your 3-question pre-entry checklist
- One line: where to learn more (your page / newsletter)

### Alternative lead magnets (pick one to start):
- **"GEX Levels Quick-Start"** — how to find gamma walls and what to do at each
- **"Pre-Market Routine Checklist"** — the exact steps you run before NY open

### Copy to use:

**The comment trigger (end of Reel + in caption):**
> "I turned this into a free 1-page cheat sheet. Comment **TAPE** and I'll send it straight to your DMs. 👇 (Follow first so it doesn't land in requests.)"

**The auto-DM that gets sent:**
> "Here's your Order Flow Cheat Sheet 👇 [link]
>
> This is the exact framework I use to read the tape on NQ every morning. If you want the daily breakdowns where I apply it live, you're in the right place — I post one every session I trade.
>
> Quick one: what's the part of order flow you find hardest right now? Reply and I'll point you to a post on it."

> The reply question matters — it turns a one-way delivery into a conversation, which feeds the algorithm *and* tells you what content to make next.
